My Current Antics

Currently I am working as a research assistant at The Fields Institute under the supervision of Dr. Deirdre Haskell. We are looking at the model theory of analytic functions, loosely following what is outlined in her paper Model Theory Of Analytic Functions: Some Historical Comments and going further in depth on each relevant historical paper. Some of the papers we have looked at so far:

  • Denef and van den Dries
  • Lipshitz
  • Cluckers and Lipshitz
  • One-Dimensional p-analytic subsets

In addition to the above I am beginning my applications to Graduate school where I am looking to continue my studies in Mathematics. While my current interests largely lie in model theory I am interested in many different areas of mathematics including:

  • (Cellular) Automata
  • Low-Dimensional and Algebraic Topology (including Knot Theory) and
  • Computability and Complexity Theory
That being said I truly just enjoy mathematics and look forward to seeing where this journey will take me.
